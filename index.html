    • Description

  • Many women value jewels, and jewels also matter to young teenaged girls like me. Before, when I was little, jewels were not of my interest. However, as my school holds a prom annually, jewels have become a tool or medium that demonstrates who I am and my own beauty. Thus, I first worked on drawing/painting various jewels that I like and created a video using the painting with myself appearing as a background in the video.
